About

Born and raised in Columbus, OH and graduate of The Ohio State University. I began my career in lawn care in the Spring of 2020 and quickly fell in love with the trade. Legacy Lawn Care Ohio was later born purely out of passion for hard work, a love of making lawns look their best, and a personal calling to glorify God through my work.

Good News

Everything we do at Legacy Lawn Care Ohio is first and foremost yielded and accountable to God. I (Eric Strubhar) experienced the love and grace of God during my freshman year at OSU by reading about the life and ministry of Jesus Christ.
